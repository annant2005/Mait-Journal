About


Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology was established in 1999 by Maharaja Agrasen Technical Education Society promoted by a group of well known Industrialists, Businessman, Professionals and Philanthropists with an aim to promote quality education in the field of Technology and Management. Since then, MAIT has grown from strength to strength to emerge as one of the top technical institutes among the private Institutes and has been constantly ranked amongst the top engineering Institutes by DATAQUEST. To build lasting relations with Industries, MAIT invites industries to join hands in fulfilling the social responsibility of imparting industry relevant technical education and training. Its alumni have also distinguished themselves through their achievements in and has been contributing significantly to industry, academics, research, business, government and social domains. The institute continues to work closely with the alumni to enhance its activities through interactions in academic and research programmes.

Computer Science and Engineering

Department of Computer Science & Engineering commits to work towards developing Engineers with a rich blend of competent, technical, managerial and social skills and contribute to nation building.Department places emphasis on all the important aspects of computers such as Computer Networks, Mobile Communication, Algorithm Design, Operating System, Advance Database Systems, Theory of Computation , Computer Graphics and many more.Department takes the initiative to improve the soft skills, analytical capabilities and verbal communication of the students so that they can face the competition in the corporate world confidently. To meet the objectives, department pays special emphasis on teaching and hands on practical work. Students exhibit their innovative ideas, skills and potentials as final year projects and have won many awards at University level.The excellent infrastructure, experienced team of faculty dedicates to strengthen effective teaching learning process ensuring quality education.We believe that this approach to teaching-learning, coupled with practical experience gained during Industrial Training in reputed organizations, equips our students to handle the challenges posed by the IT industry. Students of Computer Science and Engineering are placed with top IT companies.We as a team resolve to take the Department to heights of success and glory and prepare for the forthcoming challenges.

Computer Science and Technology

Computer science and technology departments in engineering institutions cover a range of topics including: Computer Science Fundamentals: This includes programming languages, data structures, algorithms, and software engineering principles.Computer Networks: Understanding how computers communicate over networks, internet protocols, and network security.Database Management Systems: Learning about the design, implementation, and maintenance of databases.Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning: Exploring the field of AI, machine learning, and data science.Operating Systems: Understanding the core principles of operating systems that manage computer hardware and software resources.Software Engineering: Techniques and methodologies for designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software.Hardware: Basic understanding of computer architecture, organization, and hardware components.

Information Technology

Since its inception in the year, 2001 the Department of Information Technology has come a long way. We have progressed continuously and thrived to attain the goal envisioned in the “Vision and Mission” document. With an intake of 180 students the department has currently has over 1700 students and well qualified faculty members.

Students from all over the country are imparted quality education with a vision to serve the nation with high standards of professional and interpersonal skills. Mode of admission remains highly through the JEE examination. A small percentage of management seats is filled through University and internal quality assessment criteria. The department is committed to maintain the quality of education by way of university curriculum, internal and external competition, organizing hackathons, extra-curricular and co-curricular activities, industry visits, internships and industry-academia interactions. We have a strong mentor-mentee relation by way of which every student can share his grievances with their experienced faculty mentors. Regular parents-faculty interactions are organized for bi-directional communication. We have a strong feed-back mechanism to enhance the quality of overall academic and curricular activities. We have a well planned teaching-learning process in place to enhance the learning of students as well as quality of faculty members.

Mechanical Engineering

Welcome to the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Delhi. We started our journey in the year 2019 with the Mechanical stream and have a strong undergraduate program that enables the young minds to formulate, solve and analyze engineering problems & challenges and promote student awareness for life-long learning. The department also emphasizes the students to undertake various research projects and do industrial training within four years of the study as per the curriculum of GGSIPU, Delhi. Students are placed for training in the industry and learn industrial practices with professional ethics and codes. The mission of the department is to offer excellence in Mechanical Engineering Education such that students pursue professional career or higher education for further enhancement of their knowledge. The department has well experienced and dedicated faculty members and staff with diversified specializations. Most of the faculty members are holding Doctorates and engaged in producing numerous research publications in International/National Journals and conferences, highlighting the emphasis on research and development. We are striving hard continuously to improve upon the quality of education and to maintain its position of leadership in Engineering and Technology. Our extensive state-of-the-art infrastructure including well-developed laboratories for courses like Thermal Engineering, Machine Design, Manufacturing Science and Technology, Theory of Machines, RAC, Heat transfer, CAD/CAM etc. provide unprecedented opportunities for students to learn and practice in Mechanical Engineering. The department has strong interaction with all nearby Industries and Institutes and periodically organizes workshops, FDPs, Seminars, Conferences etc. which provides additional enriched learning.
